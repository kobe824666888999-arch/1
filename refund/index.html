Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

Thank you for choosing Chic Chinese for your language learning journey. Because our studio specializes in instant-access digital services (including downloadable vocabulary sets, course curriculum, and streaming video lessons), we maintain a structured policy regarding refunds.

1. Digital Products Policy

Due to the immediate consumption nature of digital content, all successful transactions and course enrollments processed via our secure third-party payment infrastructure are generally considered final and non-refundable.

2. Exceptional Circumstances

We want to ensure an optimal learning environment. Exceptions to this policy may be reviewed at our discretion under the following conditions:

  • Verified technical errors from our distribution infrastructure that permanently block your access to the acquired curriculum.
  • Accidental duplicate purchases made on the same user account within a 24-hour window.

3. Requesting Assistance

If you encounter operational issues or believe your account qualifies for an exceptional review, please contact our support team with your digital invoice details within 14 days of the original transaction date.
