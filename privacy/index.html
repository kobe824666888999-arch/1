Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

At Chic Chinese, we are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how our educational studio collects, manages, and safeguards your information when you purchase our online Chinese language learning courses or access our digital resources.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver our digital learning services, we collect necessary account details such as your name, email address, and preferred learning goals. We do not engage in invasive tracking or sell user profiles to third-party advertisers.

2. Payment Security and Encryption

All financial operations, checkout sequences, and card details are securely managed and processed exclusively by our compliance-certified merchant of record and payment verification network. Our platform never directly records, intercepts, or retains your financial credentials or passwords on our servers.

3. Data Usage Principles

Your learning logs and email contacts are utilized strictly to facilitate course progression, provide course updates, and manage customer service operations. Data transmission across our network is enforced via high-encryption Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocols.

4. User Rights and Controls

You reserve full ownership over your personal data. You can request the extraction, modification, or absolute termination of your user account records at any time by contacting our support desk.
