Common Pain Points

Why traditional courses fail —
and it's not your fault.

音
01 · Tones & Pronunciation

Wrong tones lead to misunderstanding at work

Similar pronunciations have totally different meanings. Colleagues will not correct you actively, so mistakes repeat and affect work efficiency.

"I said the wrong word and delayed order communication."
速
02 · Speaking Speed

Native speakers talk fast in real work

You can understand slow classroom Chinese, but get confused immediately when talking with Chinese colleagues and suppliers in daily work.

"I can't catch up with fast workplace conversations."
礼
03 · Business Culture

Grammatically correct but impolite

Chinese workplace has unique etiquette, hierarchy and implicit expression. Textbooks never teach these hidden rules.

"My words sounded rude though I learned standard grammar."
词
04 · Useless Vocabulary

General words do not fit your industry

Most courses teach daily life words, not professional terms for factory, trading and e-commerce. You still cannot use Chinese at work.

"I learned many words but none for my job."
字
05 · Chinese Characters

Cannot read orders, labels and messages

You can speak simple Chinese, but fail to read WeChat messages, product labels, order sheets and simple contracts.

"I can speak but cannot read work documents."
时
06 · Limited Time

Busy work, no time for long study

You have full-time work. You need fragmented learning, focused on high-frequency sentences used 80% of the time at work.

"I only have 30 minutes per day for study."
